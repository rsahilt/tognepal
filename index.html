—The Artist ——–

"Hello! I am Saugat Rajbhandari, 30 years of age, and actively creating origami and figurines since the past 8 years, also known as The Origami Guy from Nepal. From what started as a try out to hobby, quickly became by passion. Making simple paper aeroplanes to building an almost 3ft tall human origami figurines has been quite a ride.

I have shared my origami journey, my art works and achievements here. I feel pleasure to be able to share my experience of folding small triangles of papers to building them into a art with life.

I hope after going through my works, passionates people like you get motivated in this traditional paper art originating from JAPAN. ENJOY!"
